Description

It's time to add information about the cast to our cinema. No need to delve into their biography; add a name and a photo.

Objectives
  1. Find the information about actors in a movie. You need names and photos (you can download and use them locally, or provide external links);
  2. Go to the Figma file and follow its structure. In the layout, you see seven actors, which is the maximum for our page. What if there are fewer? Foresee it, too (Example 2).
